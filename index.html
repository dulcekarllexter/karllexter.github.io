About Me

I am a third-year college student majoring in English Language Studies and I’ve always had a big love for the arts, whether it’s writing, drawing (digital or traditional), or even editing photos and videos. I see myself pursuing a career in arts or English but I’m also excited to explore different fields and discover where else my skills can take me.
