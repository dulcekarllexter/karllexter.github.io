About Me

I am a third-year college student majoring in English Language Studies. Ever since grade school, I’ve had a deep passion for the arts, whether it’s writing, drawing (digital or traditional), or editing photos and videos. Even though these are my creative pursuits, I am also eager to explore new horizons in other fields.
